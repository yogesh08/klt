Amitabh Talreja

Founder and principal designer
FROM HUMBLE BEGINNINGS TO GREAT ENDINGS.

What starts off as a bare room can turn into the home of your dreams with the right kind of contracting and designing services. And that’s what, we at H. J. Talreja, promise our clients.

With an emphasis on design thinking, we believe in developing spaces that will enhance your quality of life. With a specialization in interior design & project management, over the years, we have delivered numerous residential and commercial projects, including corporate offices, showrooms, residences, bungalows & hospitals. The emphasis in our work has always been on innovative planning to maximize space utilization & development of properly integrated systems & services into an aesthetically-designed concepts, with internationally adopted systems & standards.

Founded by principal designer Amitabh Talreja, HJ Talreja Associates started off in 2001 in Mumbai and, since its inception, the agency has designed & built many outstanding spaces that reflect a unique design vision & craft.Under the direction of principal designer, we strive to create powerful & clear design solutions individual to each project & to each client.

But over and above everything else, setting H J Talreja apart is our commitment to total customer satisfaction and strict adherence to completion & delivery schedules. No matter what the space, no matter what the need, H J Talreja will define form to create business and working environments that are expressions of excellence.

We manage the project for you from the initial space planning through product selections to hanging the last piece of artwork on your walls. We give you not only value, but also imagination.
